Freuently Asked Questions

  • What does Farmend means?

    Farmend stands for Farmer's friend.
    As a member of Farmend you are helping local farmers to bring farmer's market to your local communities and helping them to earn more, by selling their produce directly to you.

  • Do I need to order any minimum order quantities?

    No, there are no minimum order quantities. You can order as per your requirements

  • Do I have to buy together with the group in a community?

    No, Every Farmend member places his own orders, at his own leisure time through the app or by visiting Farmend website.

  • How can I return the produce if I do not like the quality or the produce is damaged

    Please talk to our customer service support and we will make sure to refund 100% of the amount we charged you for that produce.

  • When I go to Farmend homepage and enter my area pin code the page shows that there is no community in my area, what does it mean?

    It means that, there is no pick up point in your area, as nobody has started a community centre. You can either create your own community pick up site or you can choose next closest pick up site by choosing a different area close to you.

  • From where does Farmend sources the produce?

    Farmend sources all the produce directly from local farmers with best quality of produce and located within a distance of 200 miles from our communities.
    We work with local farmers to make sure the crops are grown in a sustainable way.

  • How will the produce be delivered?

    The food will be packed into individual bags after segregating, with the name card and order list pasted to the bag. Our delivery truck will drop off the bags to the pickup point.

  • Who do I reach out to on delivery day if I have questions about where my order is?

    Our Delivery Day Hotline is open for Organizers, with the most up-to date information on our trucks on the road!

  • What is a Pick Up site?

    Pick up site is a place managed by a community organiser, to which Farmend delivers your orders to.
    you can choose from the existing list, for you to pick up the produce. By choosing a pick up site to pick your orders, you help us to reduce the total cost of goods for all Farmend members

  • Where are the pick up sites ?

    Please Enter your pin code in the search box on the home page to find the closest pickup sites in your area. If you do not find any closest to where you stay you can start your own pick up site.

  • When will my order be delivered?

    Each Community has its own time range for delivery - to find yours out, just look at the top of your Community marketplace on the Farmend app/website.

  • How will my food be kept fresh?

    We make sure the produce is harvested a day before the delivery, and we take care to make sure that, the produce is stored and transported in a cool environment away from direct sunlight.

  • What if I can't pick up my order?

    You can have a family member or friend pick up your order on your behalf. If you're running a little late and can't find someone to go in your stead, just be sure to call or email your Farmend Organizer to let them know. To find your Organizer's contact information, click on your Community name.
    Please note that the Organizer is under no obligation to hold on to your order beyond the scheduled pick-up window.

  • Do you reuse any of your packaging?

    Yes!, each week please bring your bag back to organiser. We will clean it and reuse the same in our efforts to reduce useless waste

    • FAQ for community organisers

  • I am interested in becoming a Farmend Organizer, and starting my own food Community. How do I get started?

    Please visit Farmend app/website and click on Start your own community. Update the page with the required information, and our customer support executive will get in touch with you to help you to create your own community

  • Is their an incentive for the community organiser?

    Yes, community organisers will get 20% discount on food they buy for themselves and 5% commission on total sales of each week of their communities

  • What does an Organizer do?

    Organizers connect their friends, family, and neighbors with delicious, fresh food, delivered straight from local farms. Organizers need to commit 3 hours of their time once a week to run a weekly pick-up, where Community members will come and pick their deliveries. Pick-ups can be run anywhere: your home, your child's school, a community center, your office...wherever you want!

  • How will the produce be delivered?

    The food will be packed into individual bags after segregating, with the name card and order list pasted on to the bag. Our delivery truck will drop off the bags to the pickup point.

  • What is the weekly time commitment for being an Organizer?

    Organisers will need to commit at least three hours once a week on delivery day. Farmend requests a two hour delivery window where somebody can be available to receive the food each week. Additionally, at minimum there needs to be a one hour pickup window,for the community members to pick up the food.

  • What do I do if a member doesn't pick up their order?

    If somebody doesn't pick up their order, it is not the responsibility of the Organizer. You may either donate the food, give it to somebody else, or take it home yourself!

    The food will be packed into individual bags after segregating, with the name card and order list pasted on to the bag. Our delivery truck will drop off the bags to the pickup point.

  • What is the weekly time commitment for being an Organizer?

    Organisers will need to commit at least three hours once a week on delivery day. Farmend requests a two hour delivery window where somebody can be available to receive the food each week. Additionally, at minimum there needs to be a one hour pickup window,for the community members to pick up the food.

  • What do I do if a member doesn't pick up their order?

    If somebody doesn't pick up their order, it is not the responsibility of the Organizer. You may either donate the food, give it to somebody else, or take it home yourself!
